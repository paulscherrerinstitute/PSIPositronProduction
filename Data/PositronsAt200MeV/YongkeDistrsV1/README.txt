
1) Format:

  Output is in Octave / Matlab text format.
  
  A_RF and A_RF_ACC are bunches at the end of capture RF linac. 
  A_RF contains all positrons. 
  A_RF_ACC contains positrons that are finally accepted by DR.
  
  Positrons are saved in different rows.
  Columns are: x/mm x'/mrad y/mm y'/mrad t/(mm/c) p/(MeV/c)


2) Naming:

  CTSB-N02-F100-E06-S0.5-T5.0_FCTest_Pavel_SolC_CLICTW-OptionA08B7-Bc0.50.dat is for FC @ 7 T + CLIC L-band @ 0.5 T
  CTSB-N02-F100-E06-S0.5-T5.0_HTSTest_JNov04_SolC_CLICTW-Ztc200-Ri15-Bc0.50.dat is for HTS + CLIC L-band @ 0.5 T
  CTSB-N02-F100-E06-S0.5-T5.0_HTSTest_JNov04_SolC_PSISW-Ztc200-Ri15-Bc1.50.dat is for HTS + PSI S-band @ 1.5 T
